Clients Question

Navigate through our comprehensive FAQ for Clients, addressing common queries with clarity.

Our Equipment

Q: What type of hockey equipment do you provide for training sessions?

A: We provide top-quality hockey gear, including sticks, skates, helmets, and protective gear, ensuring a safe and professional training experience.

Q: Can I bring my own equipment to training sessions?

A: Absolutely! While we provide equipment, you're welcome to bring your own gear if you prefer. Just ensure it meets the required safety standards.

Hockey Training

Q: What skill levels do your hockey training programs cater to?

A: Our training programs are designed for all skill levels, from beginners to advanced players. We customize sessions to meet individual needs and promote skill development.

Q: Are the training sessions age-specific?

A: Yes, we offer age-specific training sessions to ensure participants receive appropriate instruction and guidance based on their developmental stage and skill level.

Private Lessons

Q: How do I book a private hockey lesson?

A: Private lessons can be booked through our online portal or by contacting our front desk. You can choose your preferred coach, date, and time based on availability.

Q: What is the duration of a typical private hockey lesson?

A: Private lessons typically last one hour, but we offer flexibility to accommodate varying needs. Longer sessions can be arranged upon request.

Ski Touring

Q: Do you offer ski touring as part of your hockey training club?

A: Yes, we provide ski touring as a complementary activity during the winter season. It's a great way to enhance endurance and enjoy the winter sports experience.

Q: Is ski touring suitable for all skill levels?

A: Our ski touring activities are designed to accommodate varying skill levels. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced skier, our instructors will ensure a safe and enjoyable experience.

Booking

Q: Can I book multiple training sessions in advance?

A: Yes, you can book multiple training sessions in advance through our online booking system. It allows you to plan your training schedule conveniently.

Q: What is the cancellation policy for booked sessions?

A: Our cancellation policy allows for cancellations up to 24 hours before the scheduled session without any penalty. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a fee.

Pricings

Q: How much do your hockey training programs cost?

A: Pricing varies based on the program, duration, and whether it's group or private training. You can find detailed pricing information on our website or by contacting our front desk.

Q: Are there any additional fees for equipment rental during training sessions?

A: Equipment rental is usually included in the training program fees. However, for specific gear or premium equipment, additional charges may apply. This will be communicated during the booking process.
